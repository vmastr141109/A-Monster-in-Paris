A MONSTER IN PARIS

Duration: 1h30m | 2011 | PG | IMDb rating: 6.7/10

A 3D-animated movie set in Paris in the year 1910 and centered on a monster who lives in a garden and his love for a beautiful, young singer.

A monster in Paris s a 2011 French animated musical comedy science fantasy adventure film directed by Bibo Bergeron, and based on a story he wrote.Set in Paris during the Great Flood of 1910, the story follows Emile, a shy projectionist, and his adventurous friend Raoul, whose routine delivery to the Botanical Gardens leads to an unexpected scientific mishap. Soon, a mysterious creature begins appearing across the city, sparking fear and fascination among Parisians. As authorities launch an investigation, the creature crosses paths with Lucille, a talented cabaret singer struggling to control her own future while navigating the pressures of fame, love, and ambition. With music, humor, and heart, the film weaves together friendship, romance, and self-acceptance against the backdrop of a flooded, enchanting Paris.
